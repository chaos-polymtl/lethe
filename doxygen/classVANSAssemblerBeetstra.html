Detailed Description

template<int dim>
class VANSAssemblerBeetstra< dim >

Assembler for the drag force using the Beetstra model.

The normalized drag force is computed as:

\[ F_{\text{norm}} = \frac{10(1-\varepsilon)}{\varepsilon^2} + \varepsilon^2 \left(1 + 1.5\sqrt{1-\varepsilon}\right) + \frac{0.413\,\text{Re}_p}{24\varepsilon^2} \frac{\frac{1}{\varepsilon} + 3(1-\varepsilon)\varepsilon + 8.4\,\text{Re}_p^{-0.343}} {1 + 10^{3(1-\varepsilon)} \text{Re}_p^{-0.5(1 + 4(1-\varepsilon))}} \]

The drag coefficient is then:

\[ C_d = \frac{24\,F_{\text{norm}}}{\text{Re}_p} \]

where \( \varepsilon \) is the cell void fraction and \( \text{Re}_p \) is the particle Reynolds number.

Reference: Beetstra, R., Martin Anton van der Hoef, and J. A. M. Kuipers. "Drag force of intermediate Reynolds number flow past mono‐and bidisperse arrays of spheres." AIChE journal 53.2 (2007): 489-501.
