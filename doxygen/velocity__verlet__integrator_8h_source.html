14/**
15 * @brief Implementation of a classical velocity verlet scheme for the
16 * integration of the particle motion. Note that reinitilization of force and
17 * torque is also integrated into integration class.
18 *
19 * Velocity Verlet is a second-order integration scheme.
20 *
21 * Calculation precedure:
22 *
23 * Calculation of half-step velocity
24 * v(n+1/2) = v(n) + 1/2 * a(n) * dt
25 *
26 * Updating particle position
27 * x(n+1) = x(n) + v(n+1/2) * dt
28 *
29 * Updating acceleration and velocity of particle
30 * a(n+1) = F(n+1) / m
31 * v(n+1) = v(n+1/2) + 1/2 * a(n+1) * dt
32 *
33 * @tparam dim An integer that denotes the number of spatial dimensions.
34 * @tparam PropertiesIndex Index of the properties used within the ParticleHandler.
35 *
36 */
37template <int dim, typename PropertiesIndex>
38class VelocityVerletIntegrator : public Integrator<dim, PropertiesIndex>
39{
40public:
41 VelocityVerletIntegrator()
42 {}
