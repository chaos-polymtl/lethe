389/**
390 * @brief A solver for the incompressible Navier-Stokes equations implemented
391 * in a matrix-free fashion.
392 *
393 * A matrix-free stabilized solver for the incompressible Navier-Stokes

422 */

432protected:

434 * @brief Setup the degrees of freedom, system constraints, system operator
