18/**
19 * @brief Building patches of particle properties for visualization.

22 * @tparam dim An integer that denotes the number of spatial dimensions.

29public:
30 Visualization();

32 /**
33 * @brief Build the patches of properties of particles for visualization.

35 * @param particle_handler The particle handler of active particles for

37 * @param properties Properties of particles for visualization. This is a
