Detailed Description

template<int dim>
class VANSAssemblerShearForce< dim >

Assembler for the shear force on particles.

The undisturbed shear (viscous stress) force applied to each particle is computed as:

\[ \mathbf{F}_s = -V_p \mu \nabla^2 \mathbf{u} \]

where \( V_p = \frac{\pi}{6} d_p^3 \) is the particle volume, \( \mu = \nu \rho_f \) is the dynamic viscosity, \( d_p \) is the particle diameter, and \( \nabla^2 \mathbf{u} \) is the Laplacian of the fluid velocity evaluated at the particle location.

Note
When using VANS Model A, the shear force is applied only to the particle (one-way coupling). When using Model B, the shear force is also applied back on the fluid as a reaction force (two-way coupling).
