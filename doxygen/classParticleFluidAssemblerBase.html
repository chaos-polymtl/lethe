template<int dim>
virtual void ParticleFluidAssemblerBase< dim >::calculate_particle_fluid_interactions ( NavierStokesScratchData< dim > &  scratch_data)
pure virtual

Calculates the particle-fluid interaction for all of the particles. The forces calculated on the particles is stored within the fem_force. The overall beta coefficient for the cell, which is defined as the drag coefficient divided by the cell volume, is also calculated within this function.

Parameters
scratch_dataScratch data containing the Navier-Stokes information. It is important to note that the scratch data has to have been re-inited before calling for matrix assembly.
