Detailed Description

template<int dim>
struct InterfaceTools::IsocontourBoundingValues< dim >

Bounding values of an isocontour along each coordinate direction ( \(x\) and \(y\) in 2D; \(x\), \(y\), and \(z\) in 3D). A boolean flag also indicates whether the isocontour is present in the simulated domain or not.
