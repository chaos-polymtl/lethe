Detailed Description

template<int dim>
class BuoyancyAssemblyVOF< dim >

Assembles the buoyancy source term for VOF simulations. This assembler includes density explicitly in the buoyancy term since the VOF momentum equation is written as: \( \rho \frac{\partial \mathbf{u}}{\partial t} + \rho \mathbf{u} \cdot \nabla \mathbf{u} = ... \).

The weak form assembled is: \( -\rho \mathbf{g} \beta (T - T_0) \)

where \( \rho \) is the phase-fraction-weighted density.
