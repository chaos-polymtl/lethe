24/**
25 * @brief Solver class for Volume-Averaged Navier-Stokes (VANS) equations with GLS stabilization.
26 *
27 * This class implements a computational fluid dynamics solver for the VANS
28 * equations, which are used to model fluid flow in porous media or multiphase
29 * systems where the presence of solid particles affects the fluid motion. The
30 * solver uses Galerkin Least Squares (GLS) stabilization techniques including
31 * SUPG (Streamline Upwind Petrov-Galerkin) and PSPG (Pressure Stabilizing
32 * Petrov-Galerkin) methods.
33 *
34 * The VANS equations account for the volume fraction of the fluid phase (void
35 * fraction) and incorporate particle-fluid interactions through appropriate
36 * source terms. This solver forms the foundation for CFD-DEM simulations where
37 * particle dynamics are coupled with fluid flow.
38 *
39 * @tparam dim Spatial dimension of the simulation (2 or 3)
40 *
41 * @ingroup solvers
42 */
43

49 * @brief Constructor for the VANS solver.
