Assembler for the Carman-Kozeny permeability source term used for phase change modelling.

\[ \boldsymbol{F}_\mathrm{Carman-Kozeny} = \frac{-\mu}{A_\mathrm{perm}} \left[ \frac{(1-\alpha_\mathrm{l})^2}{(\alpha_\mathrm{l})^3 + \delta}\right] \boldsymbol{u} \]

with \(\mu\) the dynamic viscosity, \(A_\mathrm{perm}\) the permeability area, \(\alpha_\mathrm{l}\) the liquid fraction, \(\delta\) a tolerance to avoid division by zero in the solid, and \(\boldsymbol{u}\) the velocity.

Parameters
[in]fluid_with_phase_changeIndicates which fluid(s) has/have phase change.
[in]carman_kozeny_permeability_areaVector of permeability area values of the solids (pseudo-porous media).
[in]carman_kozeny_toleranceVector of tolerances to avoid division by zero in the solid regions.
[in]phase_change_parameters_vectorVector of phase change parameters of all fluids.
