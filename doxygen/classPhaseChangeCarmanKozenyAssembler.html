Assembler for the Carman-Kozeny permeability source term used for phase change modelling.

\[ \boldsymbol{F}_\mathrm{Carman-Kozeny} = \frac{-\nu}{A_\mathrm{perm}} \left[ \frac{(1-\alpha_\mathrm{l})^2}{(\alpha_\mathrm{l})^3 + \delta}\right] \boldsymbol{u} \]

with \(\nu\) the kinematic viscosity, \(A_\mathrm{perm}\) the permeability area, \(\alpha_\mathrm{l}\) the liquid fraction, \(\delta\) a tolerance to avoid division by zero in the solid, and \(\boldsymbol{u}\) the velocity.

Parameters
[in]carman_kozeny_permeability_areaPermeability area of the solid (pseudo-porous media).
[in]carman_kozeny_toleranceTolerance to avoid division by zero in the solid.
[in]liquidus_temperatureLiquidus temperature of the fluid used for computing the liquid fraction.
[in]solidus_temperatureSolidus temperature of the fluid used for computing the liquid fraction.
