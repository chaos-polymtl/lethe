Detailed Description

template<int dim>
class QCriterionPostprocessor< dim >

Compute the Q-criterion scalar field within a domain.

The Q-criterion is defined as \(Q = 0.5 (||\mathbf{\omega}||^2 - ||\mathbf{S}||^2)\) where \(\mathbf{\omega}\) is the vorticity vector and \(\mathbf{S}\) is the symmetric deformation tensor.
