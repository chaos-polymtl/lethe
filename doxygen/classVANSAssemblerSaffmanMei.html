Detailed Description

template<int dim>
class VANSAssemblerSaffmanMei< dim >

Assembler for the lift force using the Saffman-Mei model.

This implementation follows the formulation in the book "Multiphase Flows with Droplets and Particles" by Crowe et al. (2011) and the brief communication article "An approximate expression for the shear lift force on a spherical particle at finite Reynolds number" by Mei (1992).

The lift force is computed as:

\[ \mathbf{F}_L = 1.61 \, C_s \, d_p^2 \, \rho_f \frac{\sqrt{\nu}}{\sqrt{\|\boldsymbol{\omega}_f\|}} \left(\mathbf{u}_r \times \boldsymbol{\omega}_f\right) \]

where the parameter \( \alpha \) is defined as:

\[ \alpha = \frac{d_p}{2 \|\mathbf{u}_r\|} \|\boldsymbol{\omega}_f\| \]

and the Saffman-Mei correction coefficient \( C_s \) is:

For \( \text{Re}_p \leq 40 \):

\[ C_s = (1 - 0.3314\sqrt{\alpha}) \exp(-0.1\,\text{Re}_p) + 0.3314\sqrt{\alpha} \]

For \( \text{Re}_p > 40 \):

\[ C_s = 0.0524\sqrt{\alpha \, \text{Re}_p} \]

where \( d_p \) is the particle diameter, \( \rho_f \) is the fluid density, \( \nu \) is the kinematic viscosity, \( \boldsymbol{\omega}_f \) is the fluid vorticity, \( \mathbf{u}_r \) is the relative velocity between the fluid and the particle, and \( \text{Re}_p \) is the particle Reynolds number.
