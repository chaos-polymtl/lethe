Detailed Description

template<int dim>
class DivergencePostprocessor< dim >

Compute the velocity divergence scalar field within a domain.

The divergence is defined as \(\nabla \cdot \mathbf{u}\) where \(\mathbf{u}\) is the velocity vector.
