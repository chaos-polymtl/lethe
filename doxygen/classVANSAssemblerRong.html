Detailed Description

template<int dim>
class VANSAssemblerRong< dim >

Assembler for the drag force using the Rong model.

The drag coefficient is computed as:

\[ C_d = \left(0.63 + \frac{4.8}{\sqrt{\text{Re}_p}}\right)^2 \varepsilon^{-(2.65(\varepsilon + 1) - (5.3 - 3.5\varepsilon)\varepsilon^2 \exp(-(1.5 - \log_{10}\text{Re}_p)^2 / 2))} \]

and the momentum exchange coefficient for each particle is:

\[ \beta = \frac{1}{2} C_d \frac{\pi d_p^2}{4} \|\mathbf{u}_r\| \]

where \( \varepsilon \) is the cell void fraction, \( \text{Re}_p \) is the particle Reynolds number, \( d_p \) is the particle diameter, and \( \mathbf{u}_r \) is the relative velocity between the fluid and the particle.
