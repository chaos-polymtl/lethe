Detailed Description

template<int dim>
class VANSAssemblerKochHill< dim >

Assembler for the drag force using the Koch and Hill drag model.

The momentum exchange coefficient is computed as:

\[ \beta = \frac{18 \mu \varepsilon^2 (1 - \varepsilon)}{d_p^2} \left(F_0 + \frac{1}{2} F_3 \varepsilon \text{Re}_p\right) \frac{V_p}{1 - \varepsilon} \]

where \( F_0 \) and \( F_3 \) are defined as:

For \( (1 - \varepsilon) < 0.4 \):

\[ F_0 = \frac{1 + 3\sqrt{(1-\varepsilon)/2} + \frac{135}{64}(1-\varepsilon)\ln(1-\varepsilon) + 16.14(1-\varepsilon)} {1 + 0.681(1-\varepsilon) - 8.48(1-\varepsilon)^2 + 8.14(1-\varepsilon)^3} \]

For \( (1 - \varepsilon) \geq 0.4 \):

\[ F_0 = \frac{10(1-\varepsilon)}{\varepsilon^3} \]

and:

\[ F_3 = 0.0673 + 0.212(1-\varepsilon) + \frac{0.0232}{\varepsilon^5} \]

where \( \varepsilon \) is the cell void fraction, \( \mu \) is the dynamic viscosity, \( d_p \) is the particle diameter, \( V_p \) is the particle volume, and \( \text{Re}_p \) is the particle Reynolds number.
