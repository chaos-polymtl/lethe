23 * @brief Identifies which type of solver is used by the DEM particles.
24 *
25 * This is used to determine which index layout of the ParticleHandler
26 * PropertyPool corresponds to which set of properties. Three solver types
27 * are currently supported:
28 * - dem: pure DEM simulation.
29 * - cfd_dem: particles coupled to a CFD fluid solver (CFD-DEM), carrying
30 * additional properties for particle-fluid coupling.
31 * - dem_mp: DEM multiphysics simulation with additional thermal properties.
