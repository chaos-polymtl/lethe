Detailed Description

template<int dim>
class PhaseChangeDarcyVOFAssembler< dim >

Assembles a phase change Darcy forcing term for a VOF two-fluid simulation. The term \(-\phi \beta_D \mathbf{u} \) is added to the right hand-side of the Navier-Stokes equations to prohibit the motion of a material. In the phase change model, the value of the \( \beta_D \) coefficient depends on the temperature field and the material (fluid) properties. Generally, this is used to impose the stasis in the solid phase. This generally leads to a better conditioning of the linear system than increasing the viscosity of the solid phase.
