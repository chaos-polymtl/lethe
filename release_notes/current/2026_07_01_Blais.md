## [Master] - 2026/07/01

### Added

- MINOR Adds a Chebyshev-accelerated inverse-diagonal smoother to the matrix-free geometric multigrid preconditioner of the incompressible Navier-Stokes solver. It is selected with `set mg smoother preconditioner type = chebyshev` and tuned with `mg smoother chebyshev degree`, `mg smoother chebyshev smoothing range` and `mg smoother chebyshev eig cg n iterations`. The maximum eigenvalue is estimated internally, and the existing `inverse diagonal` and `additive schwarz method` relaxation smoothers remain available.
