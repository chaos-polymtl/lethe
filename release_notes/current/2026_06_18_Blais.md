## [Master] - 2026/06/18

### Added

- MAJOR This PR adds continuous interior penalty (CIP / gradient-jump) stabilization as a new option for the matrix-free fluid dynamics solver, selected with `set stabilization = cip` in the `stabilization` subsection. The method penalizes the jump of the normal gradient of the velocity and of the pressure across interior faces, providing a symmetric, coercive and parameter-robust alternative to the residual-based `pspg_supg` and `gls` methods, aimed at improving the robustness of the geometric multigrid linear solver. The penalization is tuned through the new `cip velocity coefficient` and `cip pressure coefficient` parameters. [#XXXX](https://github.com/chaos-polymtl/lethe/pull/XXXX)
