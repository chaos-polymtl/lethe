### Fixed

- MINOR This PR fixes the geometric multigrid preconditioner of the matrix-free fluid dynamics solver when mortar coupling is enabled. On the coarsest level, if the level is discretized with Q1 elements, the mortar coupling is skipped and the level operator is assembled as if mortar was not enabled. The mortar contribution is therefore only neglected on this particular level. I am not exactly sure why, but this seems to avoid ill-defined mortar coupling on the coarse Q1Q1 discretization while preserving it on the finer levels. The resulting solver works well (better) than limiting the coarse discretization to Q2Q2. [#X](https://github.com/chaos-polymtl/lethe/pull/)
