## [Master] - 2026/07/18

### Added

- MAJOR A new `rbvms` option was added to the `stabilization` parameter of the Navier-Stokes equations, implementing the residual-based variational multiscale (RBVMS) formulation of Bazilevs, Calo, Cottrell, Hughes, Reali & Scovazzi, "Variational multiscale residual-based turbulence modeling for large eddy simulation of incompressible flows", CMAME 197 (2007) 173-201. It is available in both the matrix-free and the matrix-based incompressible solvers for Newtonian fluids (the matrix-based path uses a new `RBVMSNavierStokesAssemblerCore` assembler, selected like the existing `pspg_supg` and `gls` cores). RBVMS builds the stabilization parameters `tau_M` (eq. 64) and `tau_C` (eq. 65) from the element metric tensor G (eq. 66) and metric vector g (eq. 69) obtained from the mapping inverse Jacobian, making them anisotropy-aware, and adds the fine-scale cross-stress and Reynolds-stress terms (eq. 72). The existing `pspg_supg`, `gls` and `grad_div` models are unchanged. [#](https://github.com/chaos-polymtl/lethe/pull/)
