## [Master] - 2026/06/21

### Added

- MAJOR This PR adds the Continuous Interior Penalty (CIP), or gradient-jump penalty (GJP), stabilization to the matrix-free Navier-Stokes solver (`lethe-fluid-matrix-free`), selected with `set stabilization = cip`. Instead of residual-based terms, it penalizes the jump of the normal velocity and pressure gradients across interior faces; it replaces (and is mutually exclusive with) the SUPG/PSPG terms, and the pressure gradient-jump term (with a viscous floor) provides the inf-sup stabilization required for equal-order elements. Two coefficients, `cip gradient jump velocity coefficient` and `cip gradient jump pressure coefficient`, control the penalty strength. [#XXXX](https://github.com/chaos-polymtl/lethe/pull/XXXX)
