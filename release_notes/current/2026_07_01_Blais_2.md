## [Master] - 2026/07/01

### Added

- MINOR The matrix-free fluid dynamics solver (`lethe-fluid-matrix-free`) now supports the BiCGStab iterative solver in addition to GMRES and the direct solver. Setting `solver = bicgstab` in the linear solver subsection now works, using deal.II `SolverBicgstab` with the `ilu`, `lsmg`, and `gcmg` preconditioners.
